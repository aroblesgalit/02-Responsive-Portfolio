About Me

Front-End Web Developer with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Graphic Design from Cal Poly Pomona and a Certificate in Full Stack Web Development from UT Austin. Skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. Strengths in balancing design and functionality, collaboration, and time-management. During the coding bootcamp, I’ve learned a lot of things including working with other web developers from different backgrounds, communicating ideas to the rest of the team, and helping each other reach the goal. I was also able to leverage my skills in graphic design to create good user interfaces. Enrolling in the bootcamp made me realize how much I enjoy coding and seeing my designs come to life. I look forward to leveraging the skills I’ve learned to provide the best solutions to the end users’ problems.
